カルマ The Name

Part mine,
part karma

Carma is equal parts personal and philosophy.

The name is built from my own — Car from Carla, ma from Mae. I spelled it toward karma on purpose, because I believe in the idea at its heart: what you put out into the world has a way of coming back to you.

That belief shapes how the studio works. Every brand we design and every system we set up is made with intention — because careful, generous work has a way of returning the favour.

Put good in.
Get good back.
